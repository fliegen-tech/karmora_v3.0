About Us

Headquartered in Scottsdale, Arizona, Karmora is the collaborative creation of a seasoned sales professional with more than 30 years of retail experience and a group of the brightest web developers, programmers and online marketing experts across the globe. Libby McPhillips, a mother of two and grandmother to five, was still working retail in a prominent Scottsdale mall in 2012 when she realized that increasing numbers of her regular customers were electing to shop online instead of in the store by way of the merchandise they would return.

Seeing the paradigm shift in shopping habits, and tired of being on her feet 8 hours a day for nearly 30 years, Libby reached out to a close family member about creating a website where she could leverage her retail experience and replace her income from the comfort of her home. Like any good son-in-law, Mike Maksudian, who is owner of a Scottsdale based IT sales and consulting organization, stepped up to the challenge and began hiring the brightest developers, programmers, coders and internet marketing minds available to make her dream a reality.

Starting with just a few online retailers in 2013, Karmora has grown to over 2,000 stores and has blossomed into an exciting home based business delivering top Cash Back on Retail Mall purchases and high quality Exclusive Products developed specifically to help Karmora Shoppers become “Happier, Healthier & Wealthier.” From our Flawless Skin Care line to Straight Shooter weight loss and dietary supplements; From handpicked and hand crafted clothing, jewelry and accessories to Exclusive Deals; you can be certain that every Exclusive Product is of the highest quality and is being consumed, worn or used by our own families!

Libby’s dream was to create an opportunity that anyone, at any age, with even the slightest bit of internet experience could build their very own Shopping Community where they could save money, make money offering quality products, and have fun doing it. Every day our company strives to help each and every one of our Shoppers become Happier, Healthier and Wealthier! And, we are happy to say that today... that her dream is a reality!

Our Mission Is Simple

Our Mission is to provide our Shoppers with a fun and exciting environment to save and make money together as a Community! Oh yeah... and to help every single Shopper build the largest, most profitable Shopping Community in the Universe:)

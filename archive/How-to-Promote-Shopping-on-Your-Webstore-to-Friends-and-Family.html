How to Promote Shopping on Your Webstore to Friends and Family

Kim, a college student, is calling her Mom to ask her to shop on her Webstore for every day purchases so that she can earn some extra cash and not have to work extra shifts waiting tables at the local college pub. Kim has already signed up seven of her friends who are now members of her Karmora Community of shoppers and is now earning $35 per month and 20% commission overrides on seven Webstores in her Karmora Community.

Kim calls her Mom from her dorm room…

Hello Mom…

Hi Honey! To what do I owe this pleasant surprise phone call?

Well… other than to tell you how much I appreciate and love you I am calling to tell you about my new Webstore.

Awe… how sweet.  And, what is a Webstore?  Is this what I have seen you post about on Facebook?

Yep, but don’t worry Mom I’m not calling for money haha… In fact, I am calling you tell how my new Karmora Webstore is already making me money.  I’m so excited! 

That’s great!  Tell me more!

You know my sorority sister Kristy, right?  Well, the other day I was complaining about how hard it was keeping up with school and my work schedule and Kristy showed me her Webstore where she is making between $400 - $500 every month and now she has more time to study or uh party!

Well, let’s focus more on studying… but that’s sounds great.  What is she selling on her Webstore?

Mom… it’s awesome!  We have every store that I know you already shop at like Target, Kohl’s, Amazon, Best Buy, Nordstrom, 7 for all Mankind and Macy’s; plus all kinds of other smaller, less known, but way cool stores.  My Webstore has over 1,500 stores in all, representing millions of products to choose from.

OK… but how does that make you money?

Great question Mom!  Are you near a computer?  Get online and go to www.kimswebstore.com and take a look.  You’re gonna love it!

OK… let me take a look! (Kim’s mother opens up to Kim’s Webstore)  Wow… that’s your picture on the Website.  I always loved that picture. 

Thanks Mom… So, whenever someone shops on my Webstore I get paid up to 40% commission on what they purchase.  So let’s say that you want to buy Dad a new television for Father’s Day.  Type in Best Buy in the search window and now click on Best Buy.  You should now be transferred directly to Best Buy where you would buy Dad the TV.  The prices are exactly the same as if you went directly to Best Buy.  I promise… exactly the same, because you are now on the official Best Buy website.  And, when you check out I would earn a commission.

Really?  Come on… why would they pay you a commission and how would they know that I bought something?

The easy answer is that I am paid an advertising fee for directing you to Best Buy from my Webstore.  Karmora and Best Buy will know that you came from my Webstore and will credit me for the sale. 

Really… and you would earn a 40% commission?  How is that even possible?

Well I don’t make 40% on every store.  Most of the larger retail stores are between 2% and 20% on different items.  But, every dollar adds up.

So, if I bought Dad a new TV for Father’s Day for say $500 and you earned 4% you would make $20?  That’s reasonable.

Almost… I would make $16 and Kristy would make $4.   Kristy makes $5 of my $25 membership fee and 20% of the total commission earned on my Webstore because I joined from her Webstore.  And, I make $5 and 20% on everyone who signs up from my Webstore.  I have already signed up 7 people and am now earning $35 per month and 20% of the total commission on 7 different Webstores!

That’s great honey, but it sounds like multi-level marketing or a pyramid?

I knew that is what you were going to say.  I am not sure what the definition of multi-level marketing is but I am only paid on those that I sign up; I am not paid on the people they sign up.  It is just one level.

Yeah… that makes sense and sounds great.  Who have you signed up?

I have already signed up Jennifer, Paula, Steve, Karla, Diane, Dave’s Mom, and Tabitha from work.

Your cousin Jennifer?

Yep, and Jennifer has already signed up 5 people.

This is really neat, and I love the Website especially because of your smiling face.  But, I’d like to have my own Webstore.  Which would be better for you?  For me to join; or for me to shop on your Webstore?

Either way is great for me Mom.  I would love to have you in my Karmora Community of shoppers, but if you want to just shop on my site that is fine too.

OK… let me shop around and I will let you know what I decide to do.  This is really neat and thanks for showing it to me.

OK Mom.  If you decide to join, there is so much more to Karmora than just shopping and I will walk you through what I know.  And… before I go, I want you to know that Karmora gives a 30 day no questions asked money back guarantee.  If you don’t like it for any reason you can cancel your account and get a full refund.

Really?!  No questions asked?  What would I have to lose?

Exactly!  Shop with me or join my Community.  Either is fine!  Gotta run Mom.  Gonna be late for class.  I love you!

I love you too honey.

The end… Or is it just the beginning?

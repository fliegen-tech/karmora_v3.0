The Anatomy of a Karmora Sale

A screenplay written by Paula

Paula is a Karmora member with a growing Karmora Community. She has met Karla for lunch and is presenting Karmora to her friend. Paula has found great success when presenting Karmora this way and today will be no different.

Let’s watch and learn how Paula has been so successful building her Karmora Community of shoppers…

Scene 1: Paula and Karla speak after finishing their lunch…

Hey Karla… have you heard of Karmora?

Nope… what is Karmora?

It’s this great new website that for $25 you can have a Webstore with over 1,500 stores and a million products that you can earn commissions up to 40% at the same exact price as if you went direct to the stores themselves.  And I’m talking about name brand stores like Amazon, Walmart, Target, Macy’s, and Best Buy!  I know you shop online… name some stores you shop at online and I’ll tell you if we have them.

Nordstom?

Yep!

How about 7 for all Mankind?

Yep!

OK… how about Saks Fifth Avenue

Yep… we got nearly every store you can imagine!

Show me!

Let me show you on my iPad.

Scene 2: Paula pulls up her Karmora Webstore on her iPad and shows the stores…

This is my Webstore.  You can either do me the favor of shopping on my Webstore and allow me to earn a commission on your purchases; Or… you can get a site of your own and keep the commissions for yourself. Remember you will be paying the same exact price!

Well… I’d rather have my own Webstore, but if I sign up how will you make money?

That’s a very good question.  Let’s go to the “Make It” section of my Webstore.

Scene 3: Paula clicks on the “Make It” section of her Webstore and shares it with Karla…

    See, the Webstore explains everything for you.  With Karmora I have three different income streams…
  • I get paid up to 40% commission on any purchase made on my Webstore no matter who makes the purchase.  And if you sign up… so will you.
  • I will get $5 each month you remain an active member and pay your $25 membership fee.  And you will get $5 per month of every membership fee paid by the people who join your community.  Once you have 5 active members in your community your Webstore is basically free and then the fun really starts.
  • I will get 20% of the total commission paid on purchases made on your Webstore.  And you, my friend, will earn 20% of the total commissions earned by every active member in your Karmora Community.  Crazy right?  The more shoppers you have in your community the more money you can make!  This is just too easy.

So… it’s just $25 dollars a month?  What’s the catch?  There’s always a catch.  It just seems too good to be true.

No catch I promise!  Think about it… you get $5 for every active member in your community so after 5 active members your $25 is covered and from there it’s all gravy!  And, Karmora gives you a 30 day no risk trial.  If anytime in the first 30 days you are unhappy with Karmora, for any reason, you can cancel and they will give you a full refund with no questions asked!  When I heard this I was like what do I have to lose… right?!

Scene 4: Paula opens a Live Chat on her Webstore and selects “Cancel My Account” from the drop down menu…

And, if later you decide Karmora just isn’t for you, all you need to do is open a live chat and select “Cancel My Account.”  Karmora will verify that it is really you cancelling your account and honor your request with no questions asked!  Karmora makes it really easy to cancel because they truly want every Karmora member to be happy with their service!

Sounds pretty good to me.  So… the prices are exactly the same as if I shopped online by myself and I get up to 40% back… And, if any of my friends and family shop on my page I get paid up to 40%.

Exactly!

And when people sign up from my page I get $5 per month of their $25 and 20% of the total commission earned from their Webstore.

Yep!

And for the first thirty days I have a 100% money back guarantee.  And if I want to quit after the first thirty days all I have to do is call Karmora or open a Live Chat on my Webstore and they won’t hassle me?

You got it!  I’m telling you this is a no brainer!  And it’s a lot of fun.  When you start getting the commission emails for things you purchased your gonna be so excited!  But when you start getting commission emails for things you didn’t even know were purchased by people in your Karmora Community you are going to call and thank me!

Scene 5: Paula pulls out her smart phone and shows Karla her commission emails…

Look… here are some of my commission emails.

That’s awesome!  Do you get those emails everyday?

Well… every day that a sale registers from my Webstore or a Webstore in my community.  The emails only happen once a day and will be the total amount you earned for the day.

When purchases are made in your Karmora Community it takes a couple of days for the advertisers to report the sales.  When Karmora receives the reports from the advertisers they send out commission emails to notify every member who earned a commission, how much they earned and from where they earned it. 

And… even more cool is that the minute that you sign up I will get an email notifying me that you have signed up!  How cool is that!”

That’s way cool!  You said something about a Karmora Community.  What’s that?

Every Active Webstore or FREE Fundraising Webstore, that signs up from your Webstore, becomes a member of your personal Karmora Community where you receive $5 from every active member and 20% of the total retail commissions earned on every Webstore.  When you sign up, you will be a member of my Karmora Community.

Sounds like multi-level marketing?

No.  You are paid on members you directly touch.  I will not profit from anyone that you sign up, just those that sign up from my Webstore.  It is a direct sale relationship.

Yeah… that makes sense.  And what are Free Fundraising Webstores?

This is where it gets really exciting.  Karmora gives free webstores to Clubs, Charities and Places of Worship.  Think about it… one church, club or charity could represent tens, hundreds even thousands of shoppers earning you commissions.

And… it is great for the charities!  They raise money all yearlong when their donors buy things they normally buy from where they normally shop.  Take a look at this…

Scene 6: Paula takes Karla to the “Raise It” tab on her Webstore to watch the fundraising video. Karla watches the video intently…

So… I sign up my son’s traveling baseball club from my Webstore and when the basbeball club families buy stuff the team gets 80% of the commission and I get 20%?

You got it!  And… some people just donate their commission back to the club or charity.  The choice is yours.

OK… I’m in.  Let’s do it!  How long after I join will my Webstore be ready?

Your store will be ready as soon as you click join!

Scene 7: Karla clicks on the “Join Karmora” banner on her Webstore…

Let’s watch the video describing Karmora.  I know you got this, but this video is pretty cool.

The video begins playing. Paula and Karla watch intently…

That is really cool.

Now, Lets recap what you get when you join...

Scene 8: Paula scrolls through each of the benefits of being a Karmora member and stops when she gets to the viral videos…

Oh yeah… one more crazy benefit… Karmora hired the guy that won a million dollars for creating the winning Super Bowl Doritos commercial to develop viral commercials that we can simply click a button and share them on Facebook, Twitter and Pinterest to attract people to our personal Webstores.  Watch this it’s really funny and the quality is really good.

Scene 9: Paula and Karla watch the sample Ryan Anderson Viral Video on the sign up page…

That is great!  So… Karmora makes it easy to market on the internet for their members.  That is such a great idea.  I’ve never seen anything like this before!

Yep.  There are more than 10 videos you can share in the back office of your Webstore.  It’s amazing! I’m telling you… this is the real deal.  Would you like to see the videos in my back office and how they are linked to social media?

That is awesome.  I’ve seen enough.  You can show me after I sign up.  Let’s do this!

Awesome.  After you sign up you will need to secure a web address of your own and set up your e-wallet where Karmora will deposit your commissions.  Karmora has step-by-step guides and most of it is pretty self-explanatory.  I’ll help you after you sign up.  Plus Karmora is available by Live Chat or Phone if you need support.

This sounds great.  I’m ready.  Let’s do it!

Scene 10: Karla takes Paula’s iPad and signs up…

The End… Or is it just the beginning?!
